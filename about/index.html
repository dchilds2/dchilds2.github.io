We’re building a new OS for the human body

There are many internet companies that use analytics to optimize revenue or increase conversions—we've even helped create some of them. Now we want to use some of the same technology and principles to improve people's lives.

Being healthy and active shouldn't feel like a chore, or the last priority in a very busy day. We think it is possible to dramatically optimize people’s health and efficiency with well built software that helps them reach their true potential.

More than just charts and graphs, we're excited about using data to tell stories and bring people together. We have a variety of privacy options, to motivate different types of people. Some will want the default of being totally private, others may want to add friends or join a group to compete, while others may perform best when they know the whole world can view their progress.

To help people live healthier & happier lives

With new sensors & tech coming out every day, we will soon have access to everything from our realtime glucose levels to how many more minutes of sleep we need.

We believe this data should be harnessed to create a better world. It will bring us closer together. It will change how we perceive ourselves. It will save lives.
